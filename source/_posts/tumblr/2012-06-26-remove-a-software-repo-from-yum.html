I recently had to remove the Livna repo from yum because I found it was unresponsive and, more importantly, had been fused into rpmfusion.  Thus, to remove a repo, get use sudo (or su) to remove the file:

rm /etc/yum.repos.d/REPONAMEHERE.repo
